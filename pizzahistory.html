HOW IT STARTED

New York-style pizza began with the opening of America’s first pizzeria, Lombardi’s, by Gennaro Lombardi in the Little Italy neighborhood of Manhattan in 1905

Now NYC is home to hundreds of slice joints, pizzerias, and pizza restaurants serving the city’s iconic and unique pizza. The New York-style slice grew out of Neapolitan-style pizza when Italian immigrants brought pizza to NYC—and America

The mid-2000s is known as the era of the dollar slice. Some slice places started using low-quality or near-expiration ingredients to keep costs low.

A Promising Future

The first cheffy slice shop, Best Pizza, opened in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, in 2010, and it was in many ways a throwback to the classic slice places of old. More attention was paid to the ingredients and how the pies were made
